Helps you easily integrate your WooCommerce store with PointNXT.

== Description ==
You can process orders on Shopify, WooCommerce, Etsy, Amazon, Flipkart and other channels with [PointNXT](https://pointnxt.com), a multichannel order processing and fulfillment solution for WooCommerce. PointNXT automatically adjusts & syncs inventory to avoid overselling of products. Powerful features and automation are available in PointNXT's user-friendly, intuitive interface, making it simple to process orders on many marketplaces with just one tool.

Multichannel supported: Shopify, WooCommerce, Facebook, Google Shopping, Etsy, Amazon, eBay, Walmart, Flipkart.

With PointNXT and WooCommerce integration, your orders on WooCommerce are automatically imported into PointNXT for further processing and fulfillment.
